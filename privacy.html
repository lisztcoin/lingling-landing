Privacy Policy

Our app is committed to protecting your privacy. We want to be clear about how we handle your data:

  1. No Data Collection: We do not collect any personal information from our users.
  2. Local Storage: All data, including your word history and preferences, is stored locally on your device.
  3. No Third-Party Sharing: Since we don't collect any data, we don't share anything with third parties.
  4. App Functionality: The app functions entirely on your device, without sending any information to external servers.
  5. Updates: If our privacy practices change, we will update this policy and notify you within the app.
  6. Contact: If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at kaizonllc@gmail.com.

By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
